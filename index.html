About Parivahan Sewa

Parivahan Sewa is an integrated online platform designed to provide transport-related services to citizens across India. It enables users to access vehicle registration details, driving license services, permit information, and other transport services digitally without visiting Regional Transport Offices (RTOs).

The platform promotes transparency, efficiency, and ease of access by bringing essential transport services under one digital roof.

Key Services Offered

  • Driving License Application and Renewal
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC) Services
  • Fancy / VIP Vehicle Number Booking
  • Learning License Application
  • Address Change in RC and DL
  • Vehicle Ownership Transfer
  • Permit and Transport Vehicle Services

Driving License Services

Parivahan Sewa allows citizens to apply for a new driving license, renew an existing license, apply for a duplicate license, and check driving license status online.

  • Apply for Learner License (LL)
  • Apply for Permanent Driving License
  • Driving License Renewal
  • DL Status Check
  • Appointment Booking for Driving Test

Vehicle Registration Services

Vehicle owners can manage their vehicle-related services online through Parivahan Sewa, reducing the need for physical paperwork and office visits.

  • Vehicle Registration Certificate Download
  • RC Status Check
  • Change of Address in RC
  • Transfer of Vehicle Ownership
  • Fancy Number Booking

Contact & Support

For assistance related to Parivahan Sewa services, users are advised to contact their respective Regional Transport Office (RTO) or use the official helpdesk and grievance redressal mechanisms available on the platform.

Always ensure you are using official and authorized portals for transport-related services.
